How it works:

  1. Audiopod is the social music player that allows friends to create great playlists together. Just use the above form to get started! You will be provided with a hosting area to create a playlist of songs from YouTube.
  2. Friends can easily add their favorite songs from YouTube to the playlist by following the link that you provide. Any songs that they add will immediately appear in the queue.
  3. Listen and enjoy! Songs will play in the order in which they are added, and the host can remove a song from the playlist at any time.
