audiopod is the social music player that allows friends to create playlists together

How it works!

  • Audiopods are 21st-century jukeboxes. Search for songs to create a playlist from YouTube to provide the music for your next party.
  • Use the form above to name your own pod, or use the name we generated for you. (Personally, we're big fans of '{{ room_id }}').
  • Friends can easily join the pod by following the link that we provide using their phones or computers. They can then add their favorite songs from YouTube to the playlist, and they will immediately appear in the queue.
  • Listen and enjoy! Songs will play in the order in which they are added, and the host can edit the playlist at any time.
