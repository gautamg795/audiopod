Audiopod is the social music player that allows friends to create playlists together

How it works!

  1. You will be provided with a hosting area to create a playlist of songs from YouTube. Just use the above form to get started and create your own custom pod!
  2. Friends can easily join the pod by following the link that you provide. They can then add their favorite songs from YouTube to the playlist. Any songs that they submit will immediately appear in the queue.
  3. Listen and enjoy! Songs will play in the order in which they are added, and the host can remove a song from the playlist at any time.
